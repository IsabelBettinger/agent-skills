# Content Generator Skill

Generate professional, compelling copy for portfolio websites and professional profiles.

## Capabilities

- Write project case studies
- Generate professional summaries
- Create impact-driven bullet points
- Write engaging About sections
- Draft LinkedIn-ready content

## Templates

### Case Study Structure

```html
<div class="content-block">
  <div class="section-label">The Challenge</div>
  <h2>[Clear problem statement]</h2>
  <p>[Context and impact of the problem]</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  <div class="section-label">My Approach</div>
  <h2>[Methodology or strategy]</h2>
  <p>[Steps taken and rationale]</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  <div class="section-label">Result</div>
  <h2>[Measurable outcomes]</h2>
  <p>[Impact with numbers if possible]</p>
</div>
```

### Impact Bullet Points

Write bullet points following this formula:

**Action Verb + Task + Measurable Result**

Examples:
- "Increased user engagement by 45% through redesigned onboarding flow"
- "Led cross-functional team of 8 to deliver feature 2 weeks ahead of schedule"
- "Reduced churn by 23% through targeted retention campaign"
- "Automated manual reporting, saving 10 hours per week"

### About Section Generator

Prompts to use:
- Focus on what you do, not just who you are
- Include a specific achievement or metric
- Mention your approach/philosophy
- End with a call-to-action

### Hero Section Copy

```
Tagline: [Your role] + [unique value proposition]

Example: "I build the backbone of B2B companies — turning complex challenges 
into elegant, user-centered products that deliver measurable business impact."

Philosophy quote (optional): Include a relevant quote that reflects your values.
```

## Writing Tips

1. **Use active voice** - "Led" not "was responsible for"
2. **Be specific** - "23%" not "significant improvement"
3. **Show impact** - What changed because of your work?
4. **Keep it scannable** - Short paragraphs, clear headings
5. **Proofread** - Remove filler words and redundancies

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Vague descriptions without metrics
- Too much jargon
- Passive voice construction
- Listing duties instead of achievements
- Forgetting to update content after new wins
